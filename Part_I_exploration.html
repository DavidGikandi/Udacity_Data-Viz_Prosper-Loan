Introduction¶

Prosper loan is a p2p lending platform, where lenders herein reffered to as investors, offer loans to borrowers. A borrower is approaved for a loan amount depending on varaibles such as, income range, prosper score, and whether or not they own a home. The data set has 113,937 rows and 81 columns.

What is the structure of the dataset?¶

The original dataset contains 113,937 rows and 81 columns. Upon visual assessment the dataset appears to be tidy with no major issues to address at first sight.

After only considering the relevant variables we require for this analysis, the current dataset now has 113,937 rows, and 9 columns.

What is/are the main feature(s of interest in your dataset?¶

Our main area of interest is to ivestigate the relationship between Loan amount and the employment status and the term of the loan. Additionally, we will also find out how the borrower rate is affected by the term and amount of the loan.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your features of interest?¶

We exepect that there will be a higher loan amount among the employed, and higher loan ampunt will have a higher loan term. Also, we expect that the borrower rate will be lower on longer loan terms and huge amount.
